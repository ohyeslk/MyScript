Overview

multiLineView:didChangeModeWithPreviousMode:newMode:

Called every time a new mode is used. There are currently 3 modes: Writing, Correction, Insertion. The writing mode is the default mode and is also called after setWritingMode:. Use for writing texts, no highlight of word with automatic scroll. The correction is used after a gesture (except insertGesture) or a setCorrectionMode:. The insertion mode is used after a setInsertMode:, it will create a insertion zone before or after the word, according to the character index.

- (void)multiLineView:(MLTWMultiLineView *)view didChangeModeWithPreviousMode:(MLTWMultilineMode)previousMode newMode:(MLTWMultilineMode)newMode

Parameters

The current MLTWMultiLineView, managing the delegate
